# Marketing Plan – agent‑ops (90‑Day Go‑to‑Market)

## 1. Positioning  
**One‑liner**  
> *“agent‑ops: the instant‑on, zero‑config agent management platform that lets devs and sysadmins see, control, and troubleshoot every agent in seconds.”*

**Three alternative taglines**  
1. *“Agents on demand – discover, reach, and secure them instantly.”*  
2. *“From chaos to command: real‑time agent visibility for every team.”*  
3. *“Zero‑config agent ops – because every deployment deserves instant reachability.”*  

## 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

| Persona | Daily Job | Pain | Budget (annual) | Decision‑maker |
|---------|-----------|------|-----------------|----------------|
| **DevOps Engineer Dana** | Automates CI/CD pipelines, manages container fleets | “I can’t quickly see which agents are down or mis‑configured.” | $12k | Engineering Lead |
| **Site Reliability Engineer Sam** | Maintains uptime, monitors distributed services | “Agent failures slip through until alerts fire; I need instant reachability.” | $15k | SRE Manager |
| **Systems Administrator Alex** | Oversees on‑prem & hybrid infra, handles agent‑based tools | “Deploying new agents is a manual, error‑prone process.” | $10k | IT Director |

*All three personas work in mid‑ to large‑scale SaaS or enterprise ops teams (10–500+ engineers).*

## 3. Channels & CAC Estimates

| Channel | Tactics | Estimated CAC | Rationale |
|---------|---------|---------------|-----------|
| **Technical Blog + Guest Posts** | Publish 4 deep‑dive posts + 2 guest posts on Medium & Dev.to | $30 | High intent, SEO‑driven traffic; showcases technical depth |
| **Community & Slack/Discord** | Sponsor #devops, #sre channels; run AMA & quick‑start webinars | $50 | Direct reach to target personas, low friction |
| **Paid Search (Google Ads)** | Target “agent management”, “agent reachability”, “agent monitoring” | $80 | High intent, quick conversion for trial sign‑ups |

*Total CAC (average) ≈ **$53** (weighted by channel mix).*

## 5. Launch Milestones

| Date | Milestone | Deliverable |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| **D‑30** | Beta Sign‑ups Open | Landing page live, email capture |
| **D‑0** | Public Launch | Product live, documentation, community channels |

## 6. Success Metrics

| Metric | Target (by D+90) | Why |
|--------|------------------|-----|
| **DAU** | 200 | Indicates active usage & stickiness |
| **MRR** | $5,000 | Validates pricing & market fit |
| **Conversion Rate** | 5% (lead → paid) | Benchmarks CAC effectiveness |
| **Churn** | < 5% | Early indicator of product fit |
